Competencies


My day to day goes something like this...

Communication

Prior to proposing a solution, I adhere to a straightforward methodology to gain a comprehensive understanding of the objectives at hand.

Holistic Understanding: Enhance insight into day-to-day tasks and responsibilities. Explore how individuals collaborate within the organization and the interdependencies among them.

Business Analysis: Identify areas for automation, scaling, or refactoring to achieve desired outcomes for stakeholders.

Technical Analysis: Evaluate whether the existing architecture is conducive to achieving the desired outcomes for stakeholders.

Nice to Have's: Delve into additional features beyond core requirements that enhance user experience without exceeding budget or timeline constraints.

Documentation

In the initial discovery phase and pre-sales discussions, I employ a mind map format to capture comprehensive notes, conceptualizing both the scope of the workload and the existing architecture/process. Before delving into system details, I often share my screen during these sessions. This allows stakeholders to visually follow along as I create an on-the-spot mind map and flowchart, ensuring alignment and transparency.

Post discovery stage, the solution document would contain or be accompanied by a series if not all of the following:

System Architecture Diagram / Wireframes / API Mapping / Logic Diagrams / User Stories / Test Cases / Data Flow Diagram / Entity Relationship Diagram / Implementation Plan / Back up and Recovery Plan / Security Architecture

Project Support

In adherence to the Scrum methodology, I conduct bi-weekly to monthly showcases, presenting completed project milestones. This proactive approach addresses any potential stakeholder concerns, ensuring transparency and allowing for adjustments to their end-product expectations based on these regular updates. Additionally, I facilitate daily or bi-daily meetings with stakeholders to discuss and resolve outstanding concerns raised in the previous session. This consistent communication cadence helps our team stay on track with project performance, addresses any bottlenecks or dependencies, and fosters a seamless and transparent line of communication between myself and the client.
