Backup Solutions



At Captaintech, we provide comprehensive Backup Solutions to protect your business from data loss and ensure quick recovery in case of any disruptions. With our robust and reliable backup services, you can have peace of mind knowing your critical data is always secure and accessible.

Why are Backups Important


Data loss can occur due to a variety of reasons, including hardware failure, cyberattacks, accidental deletion, or natural disasters. Without a reliable backup solution in place, your business could suffer significant downtime, financial loss, and damage to its reputation.

Regular backups are essential to -


Protect Against Data Loss: Safeguard your important files, databases, and applications from unexpected events.
Ensure Business Continuity: Minimise downtime by quickly restoring lost or corrupted data.
Compliance Requirements: Ensure your data storage practices comply with industry regulations and standards.
Disaster Recovery: Facilitate a smooth recovery process in the event of a major incident.



Our Backup Solutions

On-premise Server Backup


Onsite: Store your data locally on secure servers at your business premises. Onsite backups provide quick and easy access to your data, allowing for fast recovery times.
Cloud: Securely store your data in the cloud to protect against physical damage or loss. Our cloud backup solutions provide scalable storage, automatic backups, and the flexibility to access your data from anywhere.
Disaster Recovery: In the event of a disaster, our disaster recovery backups ensure your business can quickly resume operations. We create a comprehensive backup plan tailored to your business continuity needs, including offsite storage and rapid recovery options.



Features of Our Backup Solutions


Automated: Set it and forget it with automated backup schedules that ensure your data is backed up regularly without manual intervention. Our solutions include customisable backup frequencies and retention policies to meet your specific needs.
End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring your information is protected from unauthorised access.
Incremental Backups: Save time and storage space with incremental backups that only back up changes made since the last backup.
