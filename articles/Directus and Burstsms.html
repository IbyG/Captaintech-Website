ENHANCE CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT WITH DIRECTUS CMS AND BURSTSMS INTEGRATION



At Captaintech, we are always on the lookout for innovative ways to improve customer engagement and streamline business operations. Recently, we had the opportunity to build a powerful functionality for one of our clients that seamlessly integrates Directus CMS with BurstSMS to send SMS notifications when a deal is approved. This article explores how this integration works and how it can benefit businesses looking to keep their customers informed in real-time.

Introducing Directus CMS and BurstSMS

Directus CMS

Directus is an open-source headless CMS that offers a highly flexible and powerful backend for managing content. It supports various databases and provides an API that makes integration with other services straightforward and efficient. This flexibility makes Directus an ideal choice for businesses that need a robust content management system.

BurstSMS

BurstSMS is a global SMS messaging platform that enables businesses to send and receive text messages efficiently. With its reliable API, BurstSMS allows seamless integration with various systems, ensuring that businesses can reach their customers instantly and effectively.

The Power of Integration

By integrating Directus CMS with BurstSMS, Captaintech has enabled a seamless process for our client to automatically notify their customers when a deal is approved. This integration ensures that customers are kept in the loop and can act promptly on the information they receive.

How It Works

Deal Approval in Directus CMS:

When a deal is approved in Directus, a flow is triggered checking that status field. This webhook then sends a request to a custom serverless function we created.



Sending the SMS via BurstSMS:

Using the BurstSMS API, the serverless function sends an SMS notification to the customer, informing them that their deal has been approved.


Benefits for Businesses

Real-Time Notifications

This integration ensures that customers receive real-time updates about their deals. Significantly enhancing customer satisfaction and trust.

Improved Customer Engagement

By keeping customers informed, there is a stroner engagement and the opportunity to foster stronger relationships. Customers appreciate timely and relevant updates, which can lead to increased loyalty and repeat business.
